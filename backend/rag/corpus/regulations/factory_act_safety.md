# Factory Act 1948 — Safety Provisions

Framework: Factory Act
Clause: Section 41B

## Hazardous Process Safety

Section 41B requires every occupier of a factory involving hazardous processes to provide detailed safety reports. Key provisions include:

### Section 41B — Compulsory Disclosure
Occupiers must disclose full details of hazardous processes, including maximum intended inventory, nature of hazards, and safety measures. Any modification to hazardous processes requires updated safety reports.

### Section 22 — Lifting Operations
All lifting operations require certified rigging equipment, trained operators, and designated spotters. Lifting near active process areas requires additional safety assessment.

### Section 51 — Working Hours
No adult worker shall be required to work exceeding 48 hours per week. Overtime on night shifts in hazardous process areas requires special authorization and enhanced safety measures.

## Compliance Pattern
Periodic compliance audits frequently identify gaps in hot work procedures, confined space entry protocols, and emergency response plan documentation. Continuous monitoring of compliance status is recommended over periodic audit-only approaches.
