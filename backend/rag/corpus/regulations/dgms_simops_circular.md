# DGMS Circular: Simultaneous Operations Safety

Framework: DGMS
Clause: DGMS Circular on Simultaneous Operations

## Scope
Directives for managing simultaneous operations (SIMOPS) in mining and industrial operations under DGMS jurisdiction.

## Key Requirements

### SIMOPS Assessment
When multiple high-risk operations occur in the same zone or adjacent zones, a formal SIMOPS assessment is mandatory. Prohibited combinations include: hot work with lifting operations, confined space entry with excavation, and hot work with concurrent gas handling operations.

### Communication Protocol
During SIMOPS, enhanced communication between work crews is required. Shift handover briefings must explicitly cover all simultaneous operations in the zone.

### Risk Escalation
Any SIMOPS combination involving gas handling, hot work, or confined space entry requires escalation to the plant safety head for approval.

## Near-Miss Pattern
Crane/lifting operations during process upsets or gas releases have been documented as compound risk scenarios. The failure to halt lifting when gas anomalies are detected in adjacent zones has preceded multiple recorded near-misses.
